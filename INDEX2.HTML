Beautiful Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a densely populated South Asian nation on the Bay of Bengal, known for the largest mangrove forest (the Sundarbans), a rich cultural heritage, and a strong economic growth trajectory despite challenges like climate change and endemic poverty.

It gained independence from Pakistan in 1971 and has a history rooted in the Mughal and Gupta empires, evolving into a parliamentary republic with a predominantly Bengali and Muslim population.

Capital City

Dhaka

Population

Over 170 million

Language

Bengali (Bangla)

Currency

Bangladeshi Taka (BDT)

Bangladesh is known for its vibrant festivals, delicious cuisine (especially fish and rice dishes), and the world's longest natural sea beach at Cox's Bazar. The country has made significant progress in economic development and social indicators in recent decades.
